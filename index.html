The Lynam Pups

Every family needs a little puppy running around the house. It doesn't matter if they're big, small, skinny, fat, filled with energy or just like sleepng; a little fluff ball running around will never fail to add that special something to the family tree. The Lynam's are proud to say that four of these little guys have adopted their last name over the years. Their first dog was a yorke named Rudy. After he started getting sick Chloe was added to the family to keep him company. After Chloe passed, the house felt empty without having someone to play with. Not before long the empty hole in the family tree was replaced with two yapping puppies.
